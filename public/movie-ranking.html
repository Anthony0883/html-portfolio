The Best Movies According to Anthony

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Loan Survivor

Based on true events, this intense war film directed by Peter Berg features Wahlberg as Marcus Luttrell, a Navy SEAL who was part of a failed mission to capture a high-ranking Taliban leader in Afghanistan. The film is noted for its realistic portrayal of military operations and intense combat scenes.

Con Air

It stars Nicolas Cage as Cameron Poe, a paroled ex-convict and former U.S. Ranger who finds himself trapped on a transport plane carrying some of the nation's most dangerous criminals. The convicts hijack the plane, and Poe must work from the inside to stop them without endangering his chance of reuniting with his family.

The Departed

Directed by Martin Scorsese and starring Leonardo DiCaprio, Matt Damon, and Jack Nicholson, this crime thriller about an undercover cop and a mole in the police force trying to identify each other offers intense action and complex moral dilemmas.
